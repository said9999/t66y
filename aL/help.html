PROTAG Elite is a Loss Prevention Tag.

When you leave something behind, your mobile phone will alert you.

If you lose your mobile phone, you can track your phone by the “Protag Online Tracking System”.

01.   Installation

       Download ‘PROTAG Elite’ on        Google Play Store or Apple App        Store.

02.   Register

·         Key in your email and password to register for the first time.

·         An online account will be created automatically to track your valuables and mobile phone.

·         Go to www.theprotag.com to access your account.

03.   Pairing

·         Switch on ELITE with the pin provided.

·         Place ELITE within 1 meter away from the mobile phone

·         Press the ‘+’ button on the main screen to pair.

·         You will see a new icon at the main screen upon successful pairing.

·         To setup the device, click on the icon.

04.   Basic Setup

·         Select Belongings to change the icon and name of ELITE

·         Under Distance Settings, select the preferred security distance.

SETTINGS

Mobile security settings

•        Turn on Online Tracking Features to allow your account to access your mobile phone.

•        Turn on Back Up Contacts  to allow backup of your data remotely on the online system.

•        Turn on SIM Change notification to be alerted on your preset number if your SIM Card is replaced

•        Turn on Mobile Tracking to allow your account to track your mobile phone

•        Delete Account – Unregister with the Online Service.

Other Settings

•        Select Ringtone, Vibration and enable Sound Alarm if  you wish to be alerted even if your phone is on silent mode.

•        Add WIFI zones for your phone to automatically ‘turn off’ its protection when you are in office or at home. When you leave home, it will serve as an extra reminder.

•        Note that the adjustments in other settings  will override phone’s original settings.

•        While charging, blue & red indicator on Protag Elite will light up and will change to blue when it is fully charged

 

FEATURES

1.     1. Get alerted

Get alerted prevents loss of belongings through an alarm alert. It is an automated system which recognizes wifi secured zones and switch off smartly when you are in secured zones (like your home). It will also remind you just as you leave these secured zones.

 Get alerted Setup

  1. After setup, connect with ELITE by selecting the secured  button on the belongings details if it is not connected.
  2. Select Distance to minimum.
  3. Take your phone for about 3 meters away from ELITE.
  4. You will receive an alarm notification.
  5. Select “Reconnect” to re-secure your valuables.
  6. Select “Stop” to disengage your ELITE.
  7. Select “Snooze” to stop your ELITE’s alarm for a default period of 5 minutes. 

 

2.     2. GPS tracking

If your belongings are missing, Protag Elite helps to backtrack your belongings. The GPS Tracking function records down the belongings GPS coordinates as soon as it breaks connection with the phone. You can access it at the details page of the belongings.

GPS tracking setup

  1. Check the details page for more information about your loss.
  2. Click on Lost Location to find out the last GPS coordinates of your valuables.

 

3.     3. Smart radar

Protag Elite utilizes smart radar from the mobile devices and scans up to an area of 20 metres as you walk around the area of loss. The valuables are shown on a radar map that indicates the distance between you and your belongings

Smart radar setup

  1. Click on ‘Radar’ upon reaching your location to scan and search your surroundings for your lost valuables.
  2. ELITE will be plotted onto your radar screen once your phone finds it.

 

4.     4. Protag Trace Online System

Protag Elite utilizes its online tracking system, Protag Trace, to protect your privacy. You can remotely lock, wipe, backup and track your android and iOs devices with a simple few clicks on the system. We will also include a computer automated guided system in the second phase of development for recovery. This makes tracking smart and intuitive.

Protag Trace Online System settings

  1. If you lose your mobile phone, login to the online system to track it.
  2. Track My Mobile indicates current GPS location of your lost mobile.
  3. Last Known Location displays all valuables loss information on it.
  4. Lock & Ring the phone to prevent unauthorized users from accessing it.
  5. Image capture will capture the thief image automatically if it senses the thieves motion.
  6. Remote Back-Up and Wipe secures your sensitive data.
